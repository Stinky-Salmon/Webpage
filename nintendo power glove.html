Nintendo Power Glove

The Nintendo Power Glove was released in 1989 and gained public attention due to it's virtual reality 'features'. Unfortunately, it did not stay popular for long since it's two games didn't sell well and was critisized for its difficult to use controls. It was discontinued in 1990.
